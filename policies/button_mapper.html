Information Collection and Use

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information.
The core functionality of TV Remote Button Mapper relies entirely on local, on-device processing. No accounts are required, no telemetry is sent to remote servers, and no personal identifiable information is requested.

Important: Use of Accessibility Services

In order to function, TV Remote Button Mapper requires the user to enable the Android Accessibility Service API.

Why is this required?

  • The app uses the Accessibility Service's FLAG_REQUEST_FILTER_KEY_EVENTS exclusively to intercept hardware remote control button presses (like Volume, D-Pad, or App-specific buttons).
  • This interception allows the app to block the default hardware action and launch your chosen custom action or app instead.

What is NOT being tracked:

  • The app does not read, view, or record your screen content.
  • The app does not log your passwords, text inputs, or voice commands.
  • No keystroke data is ever saved to external storage, transmitted over the internet, or shared with third parties. All mapping data is stored locally on your TV.

Log Data & Third-Party Services

This app operates completely offline. It does not use third-party analytics trackers, crash reporting tools (like Firebase), or advertising networks. Therefore, no network Log Data, IP addresses, or tracking Cookies are collected by me or third parties through this app.

However, the app is distributed via the Google Play Store, which may collect standard, anonymized crash reports and installation metrics according to their own Google Privacy Policy.
